Stephanie Cueto

I'm a Latinx Software Engineer focused in Web Development. In 2015 I decided to become an engineer. In the beginning of 2018 I got accepted into Techtonica to help me jump start my career as a Software Engineer. Techtonica is a six-month Software Engineering program for aspiring Bay Area womxn and non-binary adults. Techtonica has given me an amazing atmosphere to work alongside amazing womxn who are trying to break into the industry and become part of the community. Now going through the program, the feeling of struggling for three years to find a community that welcomed me and wanted me to succeed still resonates with me and I want to keep helping other womxn on their path into becoming a Engineer.
